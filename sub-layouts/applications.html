E-commerce - Tmall smart warehouse

The largest-scale Guangzhou Huiyang rookie Tmall supermarket wisdom warehouse in South China, which is built by Kuocang, can guarantee the business of Tmall Supermarket in Japan and the next day. The warehouse runs 200 intelligent robots, which can handle 80,000 daily. One order can handle 120,000 orders per day, and the picking efficiency is equivalent to three times that of manual work, which greatly improves customer satisfaction and makes an important contribution to improving the timeliness of e-commerce logistics.

E-commerce - eWTP Southeast Asia’s first intelligent robot warehouse

In November 2017, the rookie and Lazada and Malaysia Post’s eWTP first intelligent robot warehouse were put into operation in Kuala Lumpur. The intelligent warehousing robots enabled in the warehouse are independently developed and produced by Kuocang Company, and the robots are used to complete the automatic picking operation. Under the guidance of intelligent algorithms, the robot accurately transports the shelves, which can reduce the picking and loading of workers in the warehouse.
