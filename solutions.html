AMR - Autonomous Mobile Robot .

Priority-IS is committed to optimising the internal flows within Distribution Centre’s (DC’s) and facilities. Autonomous Mobile Robots (AMR’s) can be utilised to reduce team member walking, complete put-away tasks, reduce replenishment, speed up the picking process, and sort despatch units ready for loading. Priority-IS use quality suppliers and proven systems. QuickTron supply efficient and flexible intelligent robots to complete picking, sorting, and carrying functions. The equipment can be supplied with a highly integrated software to fully optimise your flows in the Distribution Centre and facilities.

Intelligent Picking System Solution.

The Intelligent Picking System Solution consists of mobile robots, mobile racks, replenishment and picking stations, Warehouse Management System (WMS) and Robots Control System (RCS). Al-algorithm-based software allows the solution to operate across most warehouse operations, including racking, goods sorting, replenishment, returning, inventory counting and obviously picking.

Inteligent Sorting System Solution

With the fast development of e-commerce, customers impose increasingly higher requirement of timeliness on package sorting. QuickTron independently developed QS40 and QS10 smart sorting robots which can visually identify the information on package labels. The robots can ﬁgure optimized paths for themselves before unloading packages at the right discharge point. Sorting speed and accuracy provide signiﬁcant improvement over conventional manual practice.

Intelligent Carrying System Solution

QuickTron natural navigation is supported by high precision laser positioning technology and autonomous planning capacity. It can operate soundly without magnetic stripe or beacon and can adapt itself to its surroundings by fast learning. The robot can complete predetermined transportation efﬁciently in a scenario with the presence of people and machine operation. Our carrying solutions can be adapted for numerous applications such as trolley, shelving, or pallet movements.
